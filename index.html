About me

My name is Olena. I'm from Ukraine, Kiev. I'm bubble performer. My experience around 15 years in entertainment industry. I had a lot of performances during this time on different private events, malls, parks, big and small stages, hotels, circus, street shows, festivals, outdoor and indoor events worldwide. I was a participant in shows in various countries of the world such as Oman, Kuwait, UAE, Qatar, KSA, Jordan, Korea, China, Malaysia, France, Maldives. My show has different variations and duration depending on the preferences of the clients and can be either a solo or part of a concert.
